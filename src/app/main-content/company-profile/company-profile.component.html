Living in Makati

Makati is one of the most progressive business districts in the Philippines. Majority of the multinational companies and renowned financial hubs are located in the area.

Aside from its towering skyscrapers and scenic skyline, Makati is also a home to different embassies that cater to different nationalities. It is an expatâ€™s haven.

The city is also a famous cultural hub. Different museums and galleries, which showcase world-class craftsmanship and artistry are also present.

At night, Makati transforms into an iconic hangout place for everyone. The area beams with different colors from cozy cafes, fine dining restaurants and posh bars.

For notorious shopaholics, there is no doubt that Makati is the place to be. It is just a 15-minute ride away from the 12th largest shopping mall in the world, SM Mall of Asia (MOA). Other than MOA, hotels, with great shops and finds can be also found in the place.

Convenient, safe, chic and highly industrialized, one can never go wrong in staying at Makati.
